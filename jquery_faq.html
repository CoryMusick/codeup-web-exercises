Zion National Park

  • Before it was a national park, Zion was a national monument, and it’s name wasn’t Zion. It was Mukuntuweap
  • Zion has many archeological sites!
  • Angels Landing hike at Zion is listed as one of the most dangerous hikes in the world.
  • Zion is home to one of the largest freestanding arches in the world.

Joshua Tree National Park

  • Joshua Tree National Park covers a total area of 789,745 acres
  • It gets its name from the Joshua Tree (Yucca brevifolia) which is actually not a tree at all, it's a member of the agave family.
  • Quail Mountain is the highest mountain in the National Park
  • In 1973 the album cover photos for Eagles were shot in the Park
